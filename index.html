Gushi Tang's Script List


Cleopatra

Old-school, Victorian-style Romance set in the Valley of Kings, on the banks of the ancient Nile. After an assassination attempt is made during the Young Queen's ritual morning bath, the very fate of Egyptian Civilization itself awaits a solution: who was her mysterious attacker?

Autonomous

Pimpf

But for one present day daydreamer, the night of a secret midnight "blue" performance by Martin and Lewis at the Copacabana open only to tv and advertising industry exucutives and insiders is the key

(Comedy, 44pp) August 4, 2016.
